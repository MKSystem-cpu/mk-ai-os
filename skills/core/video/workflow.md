# Video Skill Workflow
1. Device/format check only when relevant.
2. Creative Brief: objective, audience, platform, duration, style, product truth.
3. Storyboard Card + Storyboard Lock in one stage. Include varied camera angles, shot intent, composition, continuity, and QC criteria from the start.
4. Storyboard Split Prompt: use the approved Storyboard Card as the sole visual master; extract each panel to 9:16; remove panel numbering; do not redesign.
5. Voice Decision: spoken dialogue/voice-over or silent. When voice is chosen, define script, language/accent, timing, lip-sync, and audio direction.
6. Shot Sequence Overview before each prompt batch.
7. Video Prompt Generation: default three storyboard shots per 10-second Omni segment unless specified otherwise. State covered shots, progression, camera movement, timing, ambient sound, and continuity.
8. Ready-to-post social caption when requested or when it is the useful final content step.
9. Final QC integrated across all stages.

## Rules
- No separate Shot Analysis stage.
- Preserve the approved Storyboard Card through every later stage.
- Avoid repeating reference/attachment instructions already completed in Storyboard Split.
