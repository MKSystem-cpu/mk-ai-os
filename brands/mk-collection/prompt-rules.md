# Prompt Rules
Every image prompt includes camera, lighting, composition, wardrobe, palette, environment, mood, lens, quality, and negative prompt.
Default negatives: no text, watermark, logo, or typography unless explicitly required by the requested output.
Maintain face, hair, age, body proportion, wardrobe identity, accessories, lighting direction, and color grading across images.
Video prompts maintain character, lighting, wardrobe, and location continuity.
